The OpFlow preprint is now available on arXiv.

OpFlow studies robust origin-destination flow prediction under environment shift. The core idea is to separate origin demand scale from destination allocation, then learn row-centered choice potentials that transfer across changing spatial exposure states.

[Read the arXiv preprint](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.03200).
