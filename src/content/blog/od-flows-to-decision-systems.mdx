## The Bridge

Origin-destination models are often evaluated as prediction systems, but many real applications need them as decision inputs. The modeling target should therefore preserve the quantities that downstream optimizers actually consume.

## Minimal Pipeline

```text
spatio-temporal logs
  -> flow representation
  -> response model
  -> constrained decision layer
  -> monitored deployment
```

## Evaluation

The evaluation should include prediction error, stability under shifted contexts, and the quality of the induced decision. This blog category will collect implementation notes around those interfaces.
