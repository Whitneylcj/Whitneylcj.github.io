About

I am currently pursuing my master's degree in Spatio-Temporal Big Data at Peking University, after completing my bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Technology at China University of Geosciences, Beijing.

My academic background sits at the intersection of spatial intelligence and modern machine learning. On the application side, I also work on industrial decision systems, including pricing, advertising optimization, and causal decision-making.
